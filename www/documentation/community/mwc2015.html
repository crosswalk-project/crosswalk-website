Mobile World Congress 2015

The Crosswalk Project will be at Mobile World Congress.

We are looking forward to a great event at Mobile World Congress March 2-5, 2015 in Barcelona.

Two architects from the Crosswalk Project will be in attendance showing demos of the latest developments and features of the project.

If you are at MWC, we hope you'll stop by the Crosswalk Project booth inside the Intel pavillion (Location: Hall 8.1 – App Planet Stand 8.1E41 )

See you there!
